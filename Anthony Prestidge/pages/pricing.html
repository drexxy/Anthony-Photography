The Standard Package
£700

Mid-week Wedding £275

Each package consists of:
  • Pre wedding day meeting at both the church and reception venues to discuss requirements, photo locations and the arrangements for the wedding day. This may also include some early images of the various locations.
  • On the wedding day starting at the bride’s home to take images of the dress, make up and hairdressing, bridesmaids and family.
  • Arrival at the church, assembled wedding guests, groom and best man, entrance in the church and walking down the aisle.
  • The ceremony itself will be in accordance with the wishes of the clergy, respecting any conditions that will have been discussed at the pre wedding meeting
  • Signing the register and the parade back down the aisle, group shots at the locations previously agreed.
  • The wedding breakfast, reception and speeches. Cutting the cake. The bride and grooms first dance. Candid guest shots during the evening.

During the day I will be taking well in excess of 500 images of these I will select 200 of the very best and present these full high resolution images on a personalized CD. I will retain copyright but you would be free to use these for any non commercial purpose and have your own albums and prints produced. I will also provide a proof album that will allow you or your friends and relations to order professionally produced prints.

Please note that I am fully covered by professional Indemnity and public liability insurance.

Optional Extras

I am able to offer a professionally produced DVD slide show that will play on your TV the selected images from your wedding day together with background music. This will be presented on a personalised DVD complete in a personalised DVD case. The charge for this service is £100. Additional copies may be ordered at £10 each.

I also offer a range of beautiful wedding albums that are professionally hand produced abroad. Working with you I will produce a wedding album using digitally enhanced images to your exact requirement. We will agree the final design and I will have the album made, imported and hand deliver it to you.

These albums have a wide range of prices so there is sure to be one that will meet your budget.
