Welcome to Anthony Prestidge Photography

Thank you for visiting my website. I am a Wedding photographer based in Reading, Berkshire. I primarily specialise in wedding photography but also offer professional photographic services for commercial, event and portrait photography.

I offer a relaxed and contemporary style of wedding photography. Prior to the day we will meet at the venue to talk through shoot locations and the style you are looking for.

I cover the county of Berkshire, Reading, Wokingham, Caversham, Tilehurst, Bracknell, Newbury as well as neighbouring Oxfordshire, Hampshire and Buckinghamshire.
